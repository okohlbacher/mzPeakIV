<objective>
Fix two defects in rasterize.ts found by the Phase 4 verifier and Codex round2:

1. **Inferno LUT flat-top (Gap 1 / CR-01 BLOCKER):** INFERNO_STOPS[7] is [252,255,164] (same as stop[8]), creating a flat pale-yellow band across the top 12.5% of the dynamic range. Correct matplotlib inferno at t=0.875 is [249,201,52] (gold/amber). The monotonic-luminance test only samples t=0, 0.5, 1.0 and misses the defect.

2. **percentileClip formula degrades at small n (WR-02):** `Math.floor(p * (n-1))` returns index 0 for n=2, p=0.99 — clipping to the minimum value, not the 99th percentile. Correct formula: `Math.ceil(p * n) - 1`, clamped to [0, n-1].

Purpose: Restore correct inferno colormap rendering (high-intensity structure is currently invisible in top 12.5% of range) and fix silent contrast washout on small grids.
Output: Corrected rasterize.ts + extended rasterize.test.ts with regression coverage.
